About ThinkCE

Innovation. Integrity. Impact.

Our Story

Architecting the Future of Synergy

ThinkCE LLC was founded with a singular vision: to create a synergistic ecosystem where technology, media, and education converge to solve tomorrow's challenges. As a holding company, we oversee a portfolio of brands that are leaders in their respective fields.

We believe that the future belongs to those who can connect the dots. By integrating diverse industries, we unlock new possibilities and drive sustainable growth for our partners and communities.

Our Journey

A Decade of Innovation

2010

The Foundation

ThinkCE was founded as a small consultancy firm, laying the groundwork for a future conglomerate.

2015

Softivite Inc.

Recognizing the need for specialized software solutions, we established our dedicated tech arm.

2018

Creative Expansion

We expanded into the digital media space, acquiring Fsquaremedia to tell compelling stories.

2020

Eduscope Initiative

In response to global shifts, we launched Eduscope to revolutionize remote learning and accessibility.

Present

Global Connectivity

Operating across 5 continents, driving innovation at the intersection of our three pillars.
